About the Computer Science Department

The Computer Science Department is committed to developing skilled and innovative professionals in technology. Students gain a strong foundation in programming, algorithms, data structures, computer systems, and networking, while exploring advanced areas like Artificial Intelligence, Data Science, Cybersecurity, and Robotics.

Through hands-on labs, projects, and industry training, students are prepared for higher studies and dynamic careers in the tech industry. We focus on creativity, problem-solving, and innovation to empower students to design and build solutions for the digital world.

Courses Offered

  • Programming & Software Development
  • Computer Systems & Hardware
  • Data & Artificial Intelligence
  • Theory & Foundations
  • Specialized & Emerging Areas
  • Practical & Project-based Courses
